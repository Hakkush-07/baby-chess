About libaby.org

libaby.org is a free/libre, open-source chess server powered by volunteers and donations.

In 2021, Hakan and Alp began working on Libaby as a hobby project. The site was simple at the beginning, not even checking to see if moves were legal. He made the site open source, which means anyone is free to read the source code and make contributions. Gradually, the site improved and collected users as an enthusiastic volunteer staff assembled to help Thibault build and maintain the site.

Today, Libaby users play more than a million games every day. Libaby is one of the most popular chess websites in the world while remaining 100% free. Most “free” websites subsist by selling ads or selling user data. Others do it by putting all the good stuff behind paywalls. Libaby does none of these things and never will. With no investors demanding profits, Libaby staff can focus on improving the site as their only goal.

Despite Libaby's humble origins, playing a chess game is far from the only thing you can do on Libaby. After finishing a game, you can request computer analysis using the latest Stockbaby chess engine and learn from your mistakes or compare your game against a massive database of chess masters’ games. You can watch top players battle it out live and discuss the game with your friends.

A site the size of Libaby requires many people to maintain it. It also requires money to pay for servers and other hosting services. The number of people who have contributed to these things is difficult to count precisely, but they fall into the following categories:

  • Libaby would not exist without the thousands of hours of time from volunteers, both from developers, who create new features and/or fix bugs, and our administrative team, who police the site and help with long-term vision.
  • Patrons provide a steady stream of income to our site, sufficient to run our servers and handle expenses.
  • Finally, the players who come to Libaby to have fun, relax and learn, without whom this would all be pointless!
